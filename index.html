Who I am

Hi, I am Julian, born in Bulgaria with the opportunity to have lived, studied and worked in countries including: Australia, South Africa, England, Japan and Germany. I have always been looking for a creative input field and creating beautiful things on the web and mobile turned out to be exactly that. Having found my career passion, I am eager to learn, improve and develop myself in that field, staying informed about the latest tools and trends that are out there and why not set some of my own while I am at it.
